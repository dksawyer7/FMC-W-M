Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

The William & Mary Financial Modeling Club (“we,” “our,” or “the Club”) is committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of our members, event participants, and visitors to our platforms. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect personal information.

By joining the Club, attending events, or interacting with our online resources, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

a. Personal Information You Provide

  • Name, email address, and phone number (for membership or event registration)
  • Academic information (e.g., major, graduation year, academic interests)
  • Payment information (if applicable for event fees, merchandise, or trips)
  • Any information voluntarily shared in surveys, feedback forms, or applications

b. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device type, browser type, and operating system
  • IP address and approximate location data
  • Usage data (pages visited, time spent on site)

c. Information from Third Parties

We may receive member data from William & Mary’s student organization management system or affiliated campus departments.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Manage club membership and communications
  • Organize and promote events, workshops, and competitions
  • Provide educational resources and training materials
  • Process payments for club-related activities
  • Maintain records required by William & Mary or applicable regulations
  • Improve our programs and website functionality

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell personal information to third parties. We may share data only in the following cases:

  • With Club Officers and Advisors for administrative purposes
  • With Event Partners (e.g., competition organizers, guest speakers) when necessary for participation
  • With William & Mary Administration when required for compliance or reporting
  • With Legal Authorities when required by law

5. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Data may be stored on:

  • Secure cloud-based platforms (e.g., Google Workspace for Education)
  • William & Mary’s official student organization systems

Only authorized club officers and advisors will have access to member data.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for club operations or as required by William & Mary policy. At the end of each academic year, outdated or unnecessary data will be deleted or anonymized.

7. Your Rights

You may:

  • Request a copy of your personal data we hold
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to any legal or reporting obligations)
  • Opt out of club email communications at any time

Requests can be made by contacting us at financialmodel@wm.edu.

8. Use of Cookies & Tracking Technologies

If we operate a website, cookies may be used to improve user experience and analyze website traffic. You may disable cookies through your browser settings, but some site features may not function properly.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website, emails, or event materials may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

10. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any significant changes will be communicated via club email and posted on our website.
