About the Financial Modeling Club

Bridging theory and practice since 2018

Our Story

Founded in the spring of 2018 by a small group of finance enthusiasts at William & Mary, the Financial Modeling Club has grown into a vibrant community of 200+ students across all majors. We saw a gap between classroom theory and the real-world spreadsheets used by professionals— so we set out to build hands-on workshops, speaker events, and mentoring that equip every member with marketable modeling skills.

Our Vision

We envision a William & Mary where every student—regardless of year or major—can build, test, and present financial models with confidence. By fostering collaboration, peer-to-peer teaching, and connections to alumni in investment firms, we aim to launch the next generation of analysts, advisors, and entrepreneurs.

Our Mission

The Financial Modeling Club at William & Mary is a collaborative, student-led organization dedicated to equipping members with the technical skills needed to excel in the classroom, stand out in internships, and thrive in competitive careers across the financial sector. Open to students of all experience levels, the club is designed to teach the fundamentals of Microsoft Excel and financial modeling tools essential for success in the corporate world. Our mission is not only to help students build a strong technical foundation, but also to support them throughout the recruiting process and guide them as they navigate their professional journeys. By fostering a hands-on, inclusive learning environment, we aim to give every member a competitive advantage in their career path.
